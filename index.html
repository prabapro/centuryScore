DIGITAL MARKETING BOOTCAMP
This three-day workshop will help you better understand topics like marketing metrics, social media, user experience, online advertising, SEO and content marketing. Through hands-on learning, you’ll have a chance to network and put your new skills to work.
Agenda
  • Day 1, 2 & 3
  • 8am to 8:30am - Registration and Breakfast
  • 8:30am - 4:00pm - Program (includes lunch, morning, and afternoon breaks)

TOPICS COVERED
Metrics & Conversions
  • Learn skills to successfully measure marketing performance, visitor engagement, and evaluate campaign profitability.
  • Topics include: Common KPIs, Google Analytics, Attribution Analysis, ROI Analysis, A/B Testing & Facebook Insights.
Social
  • Learn how to optimize social exposure, create strategic and compelling content, understand brand awareness and measure social profitability.
  • Topics include: Content Marketing, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, Snapchat, YouTube, Social Content, Influencer Strategies, Brand Reputation, Blogging, Social Employees, and Viral Marketing.
User Interface & Experience
  • Gain the knowledge to drive revenue and ROI through user experience, attract customers through multiple digital channels, build smart site and mobile experiences, and produce rich media for marketing and advertising.
  • Topics include: Overall Digital Strategy, CRO, Web Design, Inbound Marketing, Online Video, Mobile Platforms and Location-Based.
Online Advertising
  • Learn best practices for initiating consumer interest through search keywords, targeted advertising, social advertising, and sponsored content.
  • Topics include: Google AdWords & AdSense, Facebook Ads, Retargeting, Lead Gen, Mobile Ads, Video Ads, Banners, and Sponsored Content.
SEO
  • Learn skills to optimize a brand’s visibility for search engines, improve search ranking by using optimal keywords and metadata, and use SEO to optimize positive messaging around brand experience.
  • Topics include: SEO Tools & Metrics, SEO Link Building, SEO For Localization, Keyword Strategy, Video SEO, Technical SEO, SEO & Content Marketing, and Reputation Management.
Content Marketing
  • Gain a better understanding about how creating, promoting and measuring valuable and relevant content can attract and engage a profitable audience.
  • Topics include: Lead generation, Content Marketing, Branding, Newsletters, and Blogs

Who should attend

This course is ideal for Digital Marketing managers and professionals looking to improve their online marketing performance, understand the latest best practices and insights to stay ahead.

HR & Organizational Performance

Total integration to produce business results - Strategic alignment, Culture, Performance, Employee Experience and Organizational Capabilities

This two-day workshop will equip HR Leaders with the necessary tools and skills to adapt a "human and business centered" approach to HR programs. You will learn to drive performance, enhance employee experience and develop organizational capability.

Agenda
  • Day 1 & 2
  • 8am to 8:30am - Registration and Breakfast
  • 8:30am - 4:00pm - Program (includes lunch, morning, and afternoon breaks)

TOPICS COVERED
Introduction & Strategic Alignment
  • You will learn to develop strategic initiatives and support organizational priorities.
  • Topics include: Strategic planning tools, business drivers, competitive advantage and strategy implementation.
Culture
  • You will learn to define critical behaviors, create an action plan and set the right conditions to nurture a unique culture.

  • Topics include: Outside-in value-creation mindset, strategic and operational leadership, core competencies, risk assessment, organizational learning & talent development.

You will also explore the reasons why evaluation is critical to training success and hear case studies and success stories from companies that have used the model effectively. Learning outcome

  • Objectively determine where to allocate training resources to create the most business impact

  • Define the critical difference between effective training and training effectiveness
  • Identify appropriate Blended Evaluation Plan methods and tools for any program
